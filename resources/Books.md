# GTM Engineering Books

Recommended reading list covering B2B sales automation, CRM architectures, SaaS unit economics, and scalable data pipeline designs.

## 📚 Core Reading List

| Title | Author | Focus Area | Why You Should Read It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Founding Sales** | Pete Kazanjy | B2B Sales & Outbound | The definitive handbook for engineering founders and early sales hires. Details how to run pitches, qualify leads, and build SDR teams. |
| **The SaaS Playbook** | Rob Walling | SaaS Unit Economics | Details MRR expansion, product-led growth, churn prevention formulas, and cohort retention indicators. |
| **Predictable Revenue** | Aaron Ross | Outbound Sales | The outbound playbook mapping separate SDR (qualification) and AE (closing) roles that formed modern SaaS RevOps. |
| **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** | Martin Kleppmann | Data Pipelines | Essential reading for data synchronization, ETL, databases replicas, messaging queues, and scaling GTM engines. |
| **Product-Led Growth** | Wes Bush | PLG & CDPs | Shows how to design activation triggers, map customer data platforms, and configure self-serve Stripe checkouts. |
