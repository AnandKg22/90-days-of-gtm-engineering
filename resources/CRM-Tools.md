# CRM & Customer Data Platforms

The industry standard customer relationship managers, integration connectors, and reverse-ETL pipeline engines.

## 💼 Core Revenue Tools

*   **HubSpot CRM**: API-first commercial platform popular with startups. Highly customizable properties, schemas, and custom cards.
*   **Salesforce CRM**: The global enterprise CRM standard. High-scale Apex trigger logic, object modeling, and REST composite endpoints.
*   **Census & Hightouch**: Reverse-ETL pipelines syncing analytical tables from BigQuery/Snowflake back to CRM record fields.
*   **Segment CDP**: Event streaming aggregator capturing in-app actions and routing them to HubSpot, Stripe, or Google Analytics.
*   **Apollo.io / ZoomInfo**: Contact prospecting platforms providing email, phone, and firmographic data enrichment APIs.
