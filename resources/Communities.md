# GTM & RevOps Peer Communities

Slack channels, forums, and peer networks for networking, finding career listings, and debug support.

## 👥 Recommended Networks

*   **RevOps Co-op (Slack)**: Massive community for revenue operations. Great for debugging CRM mappings and sharing compensation benchmarks.
*   **SaaS Alliance / SaaStr peer networks**: Community for SaaS founders and product marketers.
*   **n8n Community Forum**: Highly active help boards for resolving JS node issues, webhook timeouts, and custom workflow triggers.
*   **dbt Slack Community**: The gathering space for analytics engineers. Excellent for troubleshooting complex SQL window functions.
*   **Founding Sales (Slack)**: Career networking and pipeline design Q&A for early GTM hires and sales engineers.
