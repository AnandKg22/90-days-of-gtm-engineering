# GTM & RevOps Podcasts

Top podcasts discussing SaaS revenue strategy, metrics optimization, tools implementation, and startup operations.

## 🎧 Core Podcasts

*   **The Official SaaStr Podcast**: Interviews with SaaS executives detailing contract sizing, pricing tiers, commission models, and revenue operations.
*   **Acquired**: Massive deep-dives into the history of legendary companies (Snowflake, Stripe, Microsoft) detailing their original GTM sales hacks and scaling cycles.
*   **The RevOps Co-op Podcast**: Dedicated to operations, CRM configurations, data migrations, tool evaluations, and pipeline automations.
*   **Startup School (Y Combinator)**: Focuses on early sales execution, scaling customer onboarding, and building outbound SDR queues.
*   **AI in Business Podcast**: Details how enterprise teams use AI models for revenue intelligence, deal scoring, and conversational call analysis.
