# GTM YouTube Learning Channels

Highly educational YouTube channels explaining GTM business cases, SaaS scaling, and technical API workflows.

## 📺 Recommended Channels

*   **Y Combinator**: Essential startup lessons covering early customer acquisition, building minimum viable products, and qualifying corporate pilots.
*   **SaaStr**: High-level talks and interviews with VP Sales, Chief Revenue Officers, and founders detailing enterprise contracts and SaaS operations.
*   **TK Kader**: Go-to-market scaling tutorials mapping outbound campaigns, value messaging, and customer success save playbooks.
*   **Coding with Lewis**: Accessible explanations of APIs, webhooks, authentication tokens, system architectures, and back-end pipelines.
*   **Dave Gerhardt (B2B Marketing)**: Actionable breakdowns of customer research, buyer positioning, copy pitches, and marketing funnels.
