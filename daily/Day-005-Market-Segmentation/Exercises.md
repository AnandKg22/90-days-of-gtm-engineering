# Exercises - Day 005: Market Segmentation Analysis

This document contains the market segmentation model for **SmartStudy Classrooms**, focusing on private schools and test-prep coaching centers in India and globally.

---

## 📊 TAM, SAM, SOM Valuation Model

### 1. TAM (Total Addressable Market) - Global
*   **Segment**: All private K-12 schools, test-prep academies, and tutoring centers globally.
*   **Volume**: Estimated ~10,000,000 institutes worldwide.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $2,000 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$TAM = 10,000,000 \times \$2,000 = \$20,000,000,000 / year$$

### 2. SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market) - India
*   **Segment**: Private schools and coaching centers in India following recognized syllabus boards (CBSE, ICSE, State Boards).
*   **Volume**: ~200,000 institutes.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $1,500 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$SAM = 200,000 \times \$1,500 = \$300,000,000 / year$$

### 3. SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) - Launch Target
*   **Segment**: Private test-prep coaching centers in Indian metropolitan hubs (Kota, Delhi, Hyderabad, Bengaluru) specializing in JEE/NEET/Boards using digital systems (LMS like Moodle or Canvas).
*   **Volume**: ~1,000 coaching centers.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $1,200 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$SOM = 1,000 \times \$1,200 = \$1,200,000 / year$$

## 🎯 Market Segment Classifications

To optimize our sales outreach, we segment our target market (India) into three tiers:

### Tier 1: Elite Coaching Chains (B2B Enterprise)
*   *Characteristics*: 5,000+ students per batch, large budget, high reputation pressure (e.g., Allen, FIITJEE, Aakash).
*   *GTM Strategy*: High-touch sales. A dedicated Account Executive (AE) meets their directors, builds custom white-label portals, and coordinates deep engineering integrations.

### Tier 2: Mid-Size Regional Academies (B2B Mid-Market)
*   *Characteristics*: 500–5,000 students, moderate budgets (e.g., reputable local high schools or regional coaching chains).
*   *GTM Strategy*: Standard CRM demos and template contracts. We show how easily our software hooks into their existing Moodle or Canvas LMS.

### Tier 3: Local Tuition Centers (Self-Serve)
*   *Characteristics*: Small neighborhood coaching classes (<500 students) run by 1-3 teachers.
*   *GTM Strategy*: Fully automated self-serve. They sign up via our website with their credit card, pay monthly, and onboard themselves using video tutorials without ever talking to a sales rep.
