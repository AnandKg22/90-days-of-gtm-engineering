# Exercises - Day 005: Market Segmentation Analysis

This document contains the market segmentation model for VivaExams, focusing on Indian and global maritime training institutes.

---

## 📊 TAM, SAM, SOM Valuation Model

### 1. TAM (Total Addressable Market) - Global
*   **Segment**: All maritime education training (MET) institutes, simulator centers, and safety academies globally.
*   **Volume**: Estimated ~4,500 institutes worldwide.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $10,000 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$TAM = 4,500 \times \$10,000 = \$45,000,000 / year$$

### 2. SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market) - India
*   **Segment**: All DGS-approved maritime training institutes in India offering pre-sea and post-sea competency courses.
*   **Volume**: ~160 academies.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $8,000 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$SAM = 160 \times \$8,000 = \$1,280,000 / year$$

### 3. SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) - Target Region
*   **Segment**: Private maritime training academies in coastal states (Maharashtra, Goa, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu) using digital learning tools.
*   **Volume**: ~50 colleges.
*   **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $8,000 / year.
*   **Valuation**:
    $$SOM = 50 \times \$8,000 = \$400,000 / year$$

## 🎯 Market Segment Classifications

To optimize our sales outreach, we segment the SAM (India) into three target tiers:

### Tier 1: Private Elite Academies (B2B Enterprise)
*   *Characteristics*: 500+ cadets per batch, high budget, high placement focus (e.g. Tolani, AMET).
*   *GTM Strategy*: High-touch sales, custom AE demos, custom integrations.

### Tier 2: Mid-Size Maritime Colleges
*   *Characteristics*: 100–500 cadets, moderate budgets (e.g. IMSGOA).
*   *GTM Strategy*: Standard CRM demos, quick SOWs.

### Tier 3: Modular Training Centers
*   *Characteristics*: Offers 2-day to 5-day safety courses (BST, AFF, PSCRB). Small teams, very high student volume but short-term.
*   *GTM Strategy*: Self-serve onboarding, monthly billing checkout, zero high-touch sales rep intervention.
