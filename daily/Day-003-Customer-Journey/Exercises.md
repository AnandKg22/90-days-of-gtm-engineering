# Exercises - Day 003: IMSGOA Admission Journey Map

This document maps out the specific, DGS-compliant customer journey for the **Institute of Maritime Studies Goa (IMSGOA)** cadet admission and placement funnel.

---

## 🗺️ IMSGOA Cadet Lifecycle Funnel

The journey of a candidate going from an applicant to a sponsored cadet onboard a shipping vessel:

```
[ Cadet Inquiry ] ──────(App Form / Fee)──────> [ Applicant ]
                                                      │
                                            (Test & Interview)
                                                      ▼
[ Sponsored Cadet ] ◄───(DGS Medicals & Offer)─── [ Selected Candidate ]
     │
(Pre-Sea Training)
     ▼
[ Junior Engineer ] ────(Competency Exams)──────> [ Returning Alumni ]
```

---

### Stage-by-Stage Journey Details

#### 1. Awareness (Discovery)
*   **Touchpoint**: Candidate searches for "DGS approved marine engineering course India", lands on the IMSGOA homepage, or reads maritime cadet placement forums.
*   **Data Captured**: Referral keywords, device location, page views.

#### 2. Interest (Lead Capture)
*   **Touchpoint**: Visitor downloads the IMSGOA prospectus or fills the "Inquire Now" contact form.
*   **Data Captured**: Email, name, phone, engineering major (Mechanical/Automobile/Naval Architecture).
*   **System Action**: Candidate is recorded in the CRM and added to the "Admission Entrance Dates" email alert sequence.

#### 3. Evaluation (Application Submission)
*   **Touchpoint**: Candidate submits the official application form, uploads B.E./B.Tech marks sheets, and pays the entrance test registration fee.
*   **Data Captured**: Academic CGPA, physical height/weight, email verification, fee payment transaction ID.

#### 4. Selection (Test & Interview)
*   **Touchpoint**: Candidate sits for the psychometric test, technical written exam, and panel interview in Goa.
*   **Data Captured**: Test score, panel feedback, communication rating.
*   **System Action**: System filters candidates who score above the 60% threshold and creates a shortlist.

#### 5. Sponsorship & Enrollment (Closed Won)
*   **Touchpoint**: Shortlisted candidates undergo strict Director General of Shipping (DGS) medical exams with an approved doctor. Shipping sponsors (e.g. Synergy, Fleet Management) interview candidates.
*   **Data Captured**: DGS medical fitness certificate, sponsorship offer letter, admission fee payment.
*   **System Action**: Candidate is marked as "Enrolled Cadet" and seat allocation is confirmed.

#### 6. Onboarding (Pre-Sea Training)
*   **Touchpoint**: Cadet arrives at the Goa campus, receives uniform, hostel room allocation, and logs into the course portal.
*   **Data Captured**: Campus profile, biometric attendance ID, locker assignments.

#### 7. Placement & Graduation (Expansion)
*   **Touchpoint**: Cadet finishes 1-year Pre-Sea course, completes mandatory STCW courses (BST, STSDSD), and is placed on a merchant ship.
*   **Data Captured**: CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) number, ship boarding date, sponsoring vessel name.

#### 8. Returning Alumni (Renewal)
*   **Touchpoint**: After completing 18 months of sea-time, the junior engineer returns to IMSGOA for advanced prep competency courses (MEO Class IV / Class II).
*   **Data Captured**: Sea-time testimonials, competency exam target dates.
